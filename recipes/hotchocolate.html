Hot Chocolate

How to make the perfect hot chocolate for cold winter's day

Is it cold outside? Is it that kind of weather that makes you want to curl up under a blanket with a warm mug of sweet deliciousness in your hands? Then this is the recipe for you.

Ingredients

  • cocoa powder
  • sugar
  • milk (or dairy-free alternative
  • marshmallows (optional)

Directions

  1. Gently heat up the milk in a small pot on the stove
  2. In the meantime mix the cocoa powder with just enough cold milk to make a paste
  3. Remove the milk from the heat just before it boils and mix in with paste
  4. Add sugar to taste and stirl well
  5. Top off with marshmallows to treat your inner child
