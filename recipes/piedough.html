Pie Dough Recipe

How to make dough for a delicious savoury pie

This is one of my favourite recipes for creating a dough for a savoury pie. A traditional Italian recipe that will make you feel like you're back in Nanna's kitchen eating her wonderful homecooked food. Best of all, it's super easy and quick to make.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cups cold Water
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon Salt

Directions

  1. Measure the flour into a medium sized bowl
  2. Add the salt
  3. Make a well in the centre and add in the oil and water
  4. Mix well until a firm dough forms. The dough should not be flaky or sticky
  5. Refrigerate for at least one hour before use
