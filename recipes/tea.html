English Breakfast Tea

How make the perfect cup of tea just like the Englishman you've always wanted to be

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Tea Bag
  • Milk (or other Dairy alternative
  • Sugar (optional)

Directions

  1. Boil the water and prepare the tea bag in your mug
  2. Whilst you wait, whistle a tune, exclaim "by jove" and comment on the weather in a posh English accent
  3. Add the boiling water to the mug and wait for 3-5 minutes depending on how strong you want your tea
  4. You may repeat step number two at this point or perhaps sing out an off key rendition of God Save the Queen
  5. Add sugar and milk to taste
  6. Stir and enjoy with your pinky finger stuck out sideways
